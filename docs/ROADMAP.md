## ⚠️ Gotchas for a fresh session
- `podman restart mcp-proxy` after editing `proxy/app/**` (uvicorn --reload
  doesn't detect bind-mount changes on macOS podman machine).
- Never casually `down -v`/wipe postgres outside an approved verification gate.
- `tool_registry` hard-DELETE is blocked by the audit immutability guard →
  soft-delete (`deleted_at`, `status='disabled'`), unique key `(name, version)`.
- Editing `authz.rego` is a no-op until `make sign-policy-bundle`.
